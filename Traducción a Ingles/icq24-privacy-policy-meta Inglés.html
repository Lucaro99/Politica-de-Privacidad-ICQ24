Information We Collect

When you interact with our services through Meta platforms, ICQ24 INC may collect the following categories of information:

Profile
  • Name and profile picture
  • Platform-specific identifiers, such as: Page-Scoped IDs, Instagram User IDs, and equivalent identifiers provided by Meta.
Messages
  • Text messages
  • Images and videos
  • Audio files
  • Documents
  • Interactive responses sent through the platform
Conversation Data
  • Message timestamps
  • Delivery statuses
  • Read receipts
  • Conversation metadata
Page and Account Data
When a business account connects via OAuth, we may collect:
  • Facebook Pages information
  • Instagram Business account details
  • Associated access tokens
  • Authorization and connection information

When you use our services, we automatically collect technical information for security, monitoring, and support purposes, including:
  • IP Address
  • Device identifiers
  • Browser type and version
  • Server access logs
  • Connection timestamps
Technical Data: Technical data —including IP address, device identifiers, browser type, and server logs— is automatically collected when using our services for security, monitoring, and technical support purposes.
02
How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used exclusively for the following operational purposes:

  • Facilitate communication between businesses and customers through Facebook Messenger, Instagram Direct, and WhatsApp.
  • Manage, route, and deliver messages between businesses and users through our unified inbox.
  • Process webhook events and notifications from Meta to ensure real-time message delivery.
  • Manage OAuth authentication flows and maintain authorized connections between businesses and Meta platforms.
  • Provide message statuses: sent, delivered, and read.
  • Maintain the security, integrity, and availability of our services, including prevention of unauthorized access and technical monitoring.
What we DO NOT do with your data
  • ✕
    Use data from Meta APIs for advertising or ad targeting
  • ✕
    Build user profiles for commercial profiling purposes
  • ✕
    Sell, lease, or trade personal information to third parties
  • ✕
    Use the data for any purpose not described in this policy

Data Storage and Security
Secure Infrastructure All information is stored on secure servers located in the United States, Puerto Rico, and/or corresponding cloud infrastructure.
Encryption Data is protected through encryption in transit and at rest, using standards such as TLS 1.2 or higher.
Access Control We implement security measures aligned with industry standards to prevent unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or disclosure of information.
Webhook Verification Webhook payloads are verified using HMAC-SHA256 signatures to validate their authenticity.
Token Security Access to information is restricted exclusively to authorized personnel under confidentiality obligations and the principle of least privilege.
Incident Response Industry-standard security measures implemented to detect, prevent, and respond to unauthorized access.

Your Rights

In accordance with applicable data protection legislation and Meta platform policies, you have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access

Request access to the personal data that ICQ24 INC holds about you.

Correction

Request correction or update of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

Deletion

Request the deletion of your personal data from our systems. See deletion path below.

Revoke OAuth

Revoke app permissions at any time from your Facebook or Instagram settings.

Opt-out of Messages

Contact the business directly to stop receiving communications.

Formal Complaint

Lodge a complaint with the competent regulatory authority if your rights are violated.

How to Request Data Deletion
Option 1 — Automatic

Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites (or equivalent on Instagram), find ICQ24, and remove the app. This automatically revokes access and schedules data deletion.

Option 2 — By email

Send a deletion request to privacy@icq24.com indicating your account name or identifier. We will acknowledge receipt and process it within 30 days.

06
Data Retention

We retain message data and conversation history for the time required by the business relationship, contractual obligations, and applicable law.

Deletion Window: 30 days When you request the deletion of your data or disconnect the integration, your data will be deleted within a maximum of 30 days. Expired or revoked access tokens are automatically discarded. For auditing purposes, we only retain an anonymous one-way hash confirming that a deletion was processed — this record cannot be used to identify you.

Data retention periods are governed by:

  • The duration of the business relationship between ICQ24 and the company using our service
  • Applicable U.S. federal law and data protection regulations
  • The Meta Platform Terms and Meta's data retention requirements
  • Legitimate operational needs for security audit logs and incident investigation
